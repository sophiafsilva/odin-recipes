Codfish Fritter

Description

Codfish fritters are a very popular dish in countries like Brazil and Angola, imported from Portuguese cuisine.

How about learning this delicious recipe?

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg of potatoes;
  • 500g of cod;
  • 2 tablespoons of wheat;
  • Garlic;
  • Onion;
  • Parsley;
  • Salt;
  • Oil for frying.

Steps

  1. Desalt the cod: wash the cod and soak it in water and keep in the refrigerator for 24 hours or more. Change the water every 6 hours.
  2. Cook the cod for about 20 min or till it's cooked through.
  3. Shred the cod and remove its bones.
  4. Cook with salt and mash the potatoes.
  5. In a pan with oil, sauté the chopped garlic and onion.
  6. Mix the potatoes and cod, in the pan and add the wheat and parsley.
  7. After it's cool, shape it in small balls.
  8. Fry it over low heat.
  9. Enjoy!!!
