Tapioca couscous

Description

Another Brazilian recipe!!!

Tapioca couscous is a much-loved Brazilian dish. It contains tapioca, sugar, condensed milk, milk, and coconut. Preparing it doesn't require any special skills, but the flavor is heavenly.

Ingredients

  • 500 g of tapioca;
  • 2 cups of sugar;
  • 1 can of condensed milk;
  • 1 liter and 300 ml of milk;
  • 1 medium grated coconut.

Steps

  1. Mix the tapioca and sugar in serving dish.
  2. Boil the milk and pour it over this mixture.
  3. When it starts to form a paste, add half of the grated coconut.
  4. Cover it and take to the refrigerator.
  5. When cooled, serve it with grated coconut and condensed milk on top.
